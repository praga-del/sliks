📸 Imaging Protocol & Tips (expand)
📏 DistanceKeep camera 15–40 cm from web. Web should fill 60–70% of frame.
💡 LightingUse diffuse natural light or backlight. Avoid direct sun and flash.
📐 AngleShoot perpendicular to the web plane. Avoid oblique angles.
🕸️ Web TypeOrb webs (flat, circular) work best. Avoid cobwebs or funnel webs.
⚠️ BackgroundHold a dark cloth/cardboard behind the web, or shoot against open sky.
⚠️ ConditionOnly photograph DRY webs. Dew drops mimic particle deposits!
🕐 Web Age3–8 weeks old is ideal. Tap focus directly on the silk threads.
🌥️ Best TimeMorning on an overcast day gives the most consistent lighting.
